Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Short version: LUMA does not sell your data. We do not share personal information with third parties for advertising. Camera and microphone access stays on your device — your photos and videos are yours.

1. Who We Are

LUMA is a camera application developed and published independently. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

Camera & Microphone

LUMA requires access to your device camera and microphone to capture photos and videos. This content is saved directly to your device's photo library. We do not upload, transmit, or store your photos or videos on any server.

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address to identify your account and manage your subscription status. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage analytics to understand how features are used and improve the app. This includes:

  • Which camera modes are used and how often
  • Whether features such as Solo Mode or Clean Shot are used
  • Whether the paywall was shown and whether a purchase was made
  • Crash reports and error logs

This data is collected via Google Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics and is aggregated — it cannot be used to identify you personally.

Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play. LUMA does not collect or store your payment card details. We receive confirmation of whether a purchase was made in order to unlock Pro features.

Device Information

Crash reports may include device model, OS version, and app version. This information is used solely for debugging and improving stability.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the LUMA app and its features
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To send password reset emails when requested
  • To understand feature usage and improve the app
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We use the following service providers who process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase — analytics, crash reporting, and authentication infrastructure
  • Google Play — payment processing for in-app purchases

Each provider is bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Analytics data is retained for 14 months in Firebase, after which it is automatically deleted. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

LUMA is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics data collection (via device settings)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
